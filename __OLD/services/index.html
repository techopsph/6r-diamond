BUSINESS
SOLUTIONS



​

The services offered by 6R Diamond International Cargo Logistics, Inc. are designed to accommodate the individual needs of our clients and partners. The versatility of what the company is offering allows our client to negotiate and customize our service in accordance to their desired outcome by considering the optimal cost for both parties. The company offers a complete range of import and export logistic services via multimodal transportation for commercial and personal cargoes. Mentioned below are the specific services offered by the company.

​

Sea Freight Forwarding

Being a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) that issues its own bill of lading as well, 6R Diamond aims to be globally recognized as a one stop shop logistic provider that offers quality and comprehensive sea freight forwarding services to its local and international clients. Since its formal establishment in 2007, the company has maintained its vast connection of qualified partners and agents domestically and internationally.

Having an expertise in handling Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) cargoes, the company offers the following mode of services to its clients to accommodate their specific needs:

Door to Door
Port to Door
Door to Port
Port to Port

Air Freight Forwarding

Despite being a premium service, 6R Diamond upholds its commitment of providing fast, reliable and cost-efficient air freight forwarding service to its importing and exporting clients around the globe. Having a well-established relationship with its global network and agents, the company continues to provide and expand its flexible services to cater to the individual needs of its clients.

Customs Clearance and Brokerage

Having more than 40 years of experience in providing customs clearance and brokerage services, 6R Diamond has established a good rapport with various agencies, such as the Bureau of Customs and Department of Finance, to properly coordinate and address the needs of its clients. The company’s brokerage team consists of professional licensed customs brokers and customs representatives with extensive experience and training in preparing, filing and processing all the requirements needed for importation and exportation

Inland Transportation

As the company commits to deliver cost-effective logistic solutions, 6R Diamond offers a wide-range of inland transport services to its clients. 6R Diamond also guarantees to provide quality trucking services by ensuring that all of its equipment are well-maintained and, most importantly, that all of its staff and professional drivers are properly trained for the safe delivery of every cargo.
